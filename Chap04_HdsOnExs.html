THE INTERCITY WEB


THE WORLD WIDE WEB
The World Wide Web, or Simply the Web, has been a 'Killer App' of the Internet. Certainly its capability to display text and graphics and provide access to other pages and information has made it the fastest growing component of the Internet. Major online services are America Online, CompuServe and Prodigy.


GET CONNECTED
A way to access the web is to get an account with Internet Service Provider, or ISP. These accounts include accounts include access to the World Wide Web and other Internet resources, and often provide space to store Web pages one will create. A very helpful feature of the web is the capability to move from page to page by selecting specific highlighted words and prases or images, which are called as links.
